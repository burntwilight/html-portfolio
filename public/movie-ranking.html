The Best Movies According to Dakota

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bokeh

Gorgeous film set in Iceland about a couple who wake up to a world void of anyone but themselves. This film touches on a key concept of the need for community and people outside ourselves. On top of everything else mentioned, the film's score is unlike anything I have heard; it perfectly sets the melancholy feel of the movie.

Columbus

This movie is truly amazing. This movie depicts what I describe as the feeling of "midwest emo" music: a feeling of youth, love, melancholy, loneliness, and longing.

Spirited Away

One of my favorite anime films. The soundtrack is unlike anything else.
